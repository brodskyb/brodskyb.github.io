Coin Flip ++

Coin Flip ++ is a simple decision-making utility for quick coin flips, random choices, and small moments where chance gets the final vote.

What it does

  • Flip a coin quickly when you need a simple heads-or-tails decision.
  • Use it for everyday choices, games, friendly tie-breakers, or random decision-making.
  • Keep the experience clean and fast, with one-tap use and a small easter egg.

Support and privacy

For support questions, privacy questions, or bug reports, email support@brodskyb.dev. You can also read the Coin Flip ++ privacy policy.
